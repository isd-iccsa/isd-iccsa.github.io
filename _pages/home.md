There is a growing interest in exploring how computational science and Artificial Intelligence (AI) can be harnessed to address complex environmental challenges. By integrating data-driven modeling, high-performance computing, and machine learning, ISD enables innovative computational approaches for environmental monitoring, forecasting, and decision support.
{: style="text-align: justify;"}

The ISD Workshop aims to explore the convergence of computational science, artificial intelligence, environmental sustainability, and human well-being, emphasizing how intelligent computational models can contribute to both planetary and public health. The main themes include:
{: style="text-align: justify;"}

- AI and Computational Modeling for Sustainability, Health, and Elderly Care;
- Machine Learning and Remote Sensing for Environmental Monitoring;
- Digital Twins and Simulation of Natural and Urban Systems;
- Data Fusion and Big Data Analytics for ISD;
- AI for Renewable Energy, Climate, and Sustainable Cities;
- Explainable, Ethical, and Trustworthy AI for ISD;
- Reproducible and Open Computational Frameworks for ISD;
- Cross-domain Applications for ISD.
